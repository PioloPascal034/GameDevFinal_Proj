Fragment 3 — “The Escape”
He escapes from his capsule in a panic.
Alarms ring. Red lights flash everywhere.
Hollowed test subjects break loose and attack the scientists.
He tries to run but an explosion rips the lab apart.
He barely survives and stumbles out into chaos.
He wakes up from the recollection and gathers:
“When I escaped… the outbreak escaped with me.”
